Service

Service is one secret to our success. Since KNOLL products are used around the world, we offer you this service around the world. We will assist you in timely fashion and provide conclusive diagnoses. If necessary, we will send you our service technicians on short notice. In our spare parts warehouses, we stock the most common wearing and spare parts and consumables. They can be called up at any time – for quick shipping and prompt use.

Our services

Our KNOLL service includes a varied spectrum of services. Services range from the hotline to remote maintenance to on-site support. Of course our experts will also make modifications, do repairs and optimize your systems.

Assembly

add_circle
remove_circle

We will handle proper system set-up for you. This saves you time and you do not have to provide any personnel. Thanks to our many years' experience, errors in assembly are nearly excluded. The system will be ready for operation in the shortest time.

Description of services 

  • On your premises or in the end customer's plant
  • With German and English-speaking personnel
  • Building site management
  • Set-up and assembly of the system components
  • Welding of the safety basin
  • Cabling of the consumers in the switch cabinet

Contact

Startup

add_circle
remove_circle

We will start up your KNOLL system and ensure that it is ready for production start-up without delay. If faults occur, our technicians will remedy these immediately. We will guarantee full functionality and with the start-up of the system, we lay the basis for optimal utilization. Of course we will also handle technical support on-site.

Description of services

  • Checking of the mechanical and electrical components of the KNOLL system
  • Checking to ensure completeness
  • Establishment of system readiness
  • Start-up assistance
  • Fine adjustment of the system in the ongoing manufacturing process
  • Elimination of faults
  • Operator training and safety training

Energy optimization

add_circle
remove_circle

Our KNOLL experts are in a position to optimize existing systems with respect to energy consumption and to save up to 70% of the energy costs. Other advantages: : Less heat transfer to the medium, reduced wear and maintenance effort, minimized noise, The amortization time is short. Financial support is offered by a subsidy program of the German Federal Office of Economics and Export Control. Furthermore, the CO2 reduction makes an important contribution to environmental protection.

Description of services

  • On-site service
  • Pre-conference for estimation of potential
  • All KTS high-pressure pumps can be checked
  • Measurement in the ongoing production process
  • Creation of an energy log
  • Quotation with cost/benefit comparison and amortization time for upgrade to PQ-Tronic control technology 

Contact

Laboratory analysis

add_circle
remove_circle

We will check the quality of your cooling lubricant, for insufficient quality causes breakdowns and compromises productivity. Faults can be limited and eliminated with our specialized tests. This is one way that you can ensure the high productivity of your system. Our laboratory analysis is also suitable for identifying modifications or new purchases of the best possible filtering technology.

Description of services

  • Examination of dirt content, particle size, and filter capacity
  • Provision of test sampling equipment
  • Assistance with test sampling by technical support
  • Laboratory results report
