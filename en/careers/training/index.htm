We will enhance you

You're at the start of your professional development and want to know more? What input can I give? What does my future employer has to offer? At KNOLL, you're spot-on if you want to develop your skills and abilities into solid technical knowledge. We don't just enhance chips and things, but above all you! At KNOLL, you will receive sound training with super perspectives for being taken on afterwards. We train according to our needs, not just for the sake of it.

Occupations requiring training

The first job should be the right one. At KNOLL you will start with good experience: We can offer you technical expertise and state-of-the-art practical experience in a youthful environment with many other trainees. Naturally, we would like to see a good school-leaving certificate, but grades are not everything. If you can convince us to recruit you and if you perform well in the examination, you will have a good chance of being given a firm job offer.

Bachelor courses

You don't want to swot on theory for ever. You'd much rather look for a practically oriented course of study that includes lots of training "on the job". We can offer you four Bachelor courses. In each, you will gain deep insight into the work of our departments and get to know their specific fields of work. After three years, you will be fit enough to take on greater responsibility.
