Training at KNOLL

We will enhance you

The issue of enhancement is a matter that we take seriously. Not just with our conveyor and filtration systems. As a trainee, we will prepare you in theory and practice for the challenges of your job. From the very first day, you will be involved in our processes and you will soon be taking responsibility. 

Occupations requiring training

As a company, we are growing, so we always need reinforcements in the form of up-and-coming talents. It doesn't matter whether you have a technical, electronic or commercial/administrative background; the opportunities at KNOLL are diverse and there's sure something for you too. Take a closer look at our occupations requiring training. We look forward to hearing from you!

Application process

Now it's even easier to apply: Send us your video application via the JobUfo app! Of course, you can also apply online, by mail or e-mail. We will examine your documents and – assuming you fit into our profile – invite you to an aptitude test, where we will find out more about your professional and personal skills. The next step will then be a personal interview. And then it's a matter of keeping your fingers crossed!

For information: The test is conducted entirely on a PC. No additional documents are needed.

Video application: Simply download the app, register, search for KNOLL Bad Saulgau and off you go!
