Careers at KNOLL

To the job vacanciesarrow_forward

KNOLL unites the innovative potential of a global market leader with the warmheartedness of a medium-sized family business. That characterises the atmosphere in our company and the way people pursue a career with us. We cultivate diversity, value the effort of the individual and enhance your professional development. Around 1000 employees at the site in Bad Saulgau develop their individual talents and skills, regardless of whether they are career starters or technically qualified staff.

How we tick

Our employees want to progress. They enjoy taking responsibility, varied tasks and like to tackle new things. Besides career goals, personal goals do not get neglected at KNOLL. We create the conditions needed for professional and private life to stay in balance, meaning you don't have to compromise. Growth and security, internationality and strong regional ties – for us that is all inseparable.

Our working environment

Career perspectives and a workplace with modern equipment are important to make you feel comfortable in your working environment. At KNOLL, that's not all that counts. We offer a relaxed environment for collegial exchanges and shared activities. At the end of the day, the best ideas are often thought of in an informal situation – and in addition the sense of togetherness is also reinforced.

Our added value

At KNOLL, we will always see you as a person, not just as an employee. With numerous added values in the fields of health, social issues, quality of life and quality of work, we do a lot to remain attractive as an employer. The offers are constantly being expanded and adjusted to reflect the latest social developments. Find out what you can expect from us!

Flexitime and overtime arrangements

add_circle
remove_circle

At KNOLL, we have core working times, but you are free to decide when you start and when you finish your daily work – of course in agreement with your supervisor and/or your team. That makes it easier to flexibly plan personal appointments and organise family life. Overtime can be taken in the form of time off in lieu or, if necessary, can be paid, in agreement.

Friendly working environment

add_circle
remove_circle

Modern equipment and a pleasant climate await you at your workplace. Bright rooms, flooded with daylight are standard for us, even in the production halls. Ergonomics and back-friendliness are extremely important to us. This is demonstrated by the soft floor linings for working standing up and stand-up workplaces in the offices.

Healthy with KNOLL

add_circle
remove_circle

We have installed a comprehensive active and prevention offer to promote the health of our employees. This includes, for example, the company's own sports offerings such as spinning and yoga, and team days with canoeing and climbing events. Here, we work with fitness studios and other experienced partners in the region

KNOLL Social

add_circle
remove_circle

We are firmly rooted in the region and we accept our social responsibility. The company's founder, Walter Knoll, once laid the basis for this with the Bad Saulgau Civic Trust. Here, we are involved in youth support and care for the elderly, we assist social insitutions in the region, and support the preservation of historical monuments, to name just a few examples. We operate an exchange program with a disabled workshop through the KNOLL inclusion project with the OWB and we help to provide an answer to current challenges for our society by integrating refugees.

KNOLL open

add_circle
remove_circle

We don't just do sports ourselves; we are also the main sponsor and the name giver of the KNOLL Open ladies world-ranking tennis tournament, which is known well beyond the region. This is where internationally well-known names from the world of tennis meet. An attractive fringe program makes the tournament a popular event.

Job security

add_circle
remove_circle

KNOLL stands for continuous development and expansion of its fields of business. Our company is built on a solid economic foundation and is growing on its own. Sustainable growth is more important to us than a fast profit, and this enables us to offer our employees secure long-term job perspectives.

Further training

add_circle
remove_circle

We believe that a company's structure should develop organically and that people who have been part of our team for a long time simply understand and convey our corporate culture best. Even experienced specialists never stop learning. We will promote the next step in your career with intensive internal and external further training activities such as master tradesmen and technician courses, business administrator training and specialist training activities. Our exclusive KNOLL Talent Pool offers individual promotion for members of staff in all positions and sections of the company. That's why we are so pleased to welcome new members of staff who are also interested in long-term collaboration.

Vocational training

add_circle
remove_circle

KNOLL is a recognised training organisation and have been awarded with the official seal "Training Ace". We give young employees the chance to lay a valuable groundstone for their later professional life – with excellent chances of being taken on afterwards, assuming your final certificate and personal commitment are as expected. We place lots of trust in "home-grown talent" from within the company. It often happens that a member of staff started work with us as a trainee and is now a member of our management staff.

Current job vacancies

You're looking for an exciting working environment but you don't want to neglect your personal development? At KNOLL, you are exactly at the right place then. We are looking for solution providers for development and production, service staff, sales agents and administration professionals. Find the profile that suits you best here!
